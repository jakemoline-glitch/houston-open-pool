Pool Rules

1
Entry Fee: $50 per entry, paid via Venmo to @Jake-Moline-1.
2
Pick Format: Select 1 golfer from each of the 6 groups, plus 3 wild card picks from any group. That gives you 9 total golfers.
3
Scoring: Your score is the sum of your best 6 out of 9 golfers' scores (to par). Lower is better. Wild card picks are eligible for the best-6 count.
4
Missed Cut: Any golfer who misses the cut receives a score of +10 for scoring purposes.
5
Tiebreaker: In the event of a tie, the participant whose predicted winning score is closest to the actual winning score wins. No tiebreaker = eliminated from tiebreak consideration.
6
Pick Lock: All picks must be submitted before Thursday, March 26 at 8:00 AM ET. No changes after lock.
7
Payout: Prize structure announced once all entries are in.
